From the perspective of the creator, I want to define this project as a "story serialized publication," and It summarizes our society as Its name has shown, Flood. For this work, firstly, I observe some phenomenons in current society, then create a script, and use it as the guideline and structure to produce the shot list and shoot the video clips. After the shooting, I decide not to edit the video material, but make the screenshot from them to emphasize the critical elements in this storyline. Furthermore, by organizing and composing, I create new visual images. For the audience, The strategy is displaying only these visual images, then the video material and the sound material are placed on a website, and the materials are not open to the audience in a short time. Through the elements of the visual image, the audience creates a story in their minds.

For example, we mentioned: man, apple tree, fallen apple. Most viewers will associate Newton (already verified). Then over time, the story will get a relatively unified version. Based on this version, we edited the material on the website to create a story of this "unified version." The reason why do I want to call this piece a publication or newspapers is because I want to make this project a long-term, serial-loaded work. This kind of story will come out every three months, and the initial plan is to do it for three years. The themes of these stories are all from original life. They are real. We live in similar stories every day. The protagonist in the story does not reveal his face because this protagonist does not refer to a specific person. He or she can be you, me, or anyone in the world.
